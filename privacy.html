5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS 1.2+ encryption;
  • Encryption at Rest: Data stored in our databases is encrypted using AES-256;
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with individual salts;
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access controls for our systems;
  • Infrastructure Security: Hosted on Google Cloud Platform with enterprise-grade security;
  • Monitoring: Automated monitoring for security threats and suspicious activity;
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security reviews and vulnerability assessments.

5.2 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials. We recommend:

5.3 Data Breach Notification

9.3 Your Choices

  • Browser Cookies: Configure your browser to block or delete cookies;
  • Mobile Advertising ID: Reset or limit ad tracking in your device settings:
    • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
    • Android: Settings > Privacy > Ads
  • App Tracking Transparency (iOS): When prompted, choose whether to allow tracking;
  • Premium Subscription: Upgrade to premium to disable all advertising and ad-related tracking.

9.4 Tracking and Advertising Transparency

On iOS devices, RecipeAI requests your permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. This permission is requested before displaying your first advertisement.

What Tracking Means

If you grant permission, the Google AdMob SDK integrated within RecipeAI may access your device's Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) and combine it with data collected from other apps and websites to:

  • Deliver advertisements that are more relevant to your interests;
  • Measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns;
  • Attribute ad interactions and conversions across apps and websites.

If You Decline Tracking

If you decline or have not yet been asked, RecipeAI will still display advertisements; however, those advertisements will be non-personalized and not based on your activity across other apps and websites. Declining tracking does not affect any other functionality of the Service.

Third-Party Advertising Partners

RecipeAI uses the Google AdMob SDK, which may share device identifiers and ad interaction data with Google's advertising network and its demand partners for the purposes described above. For a complete list of Google's advertising partners and their respective privacy policies, please visit Google's Partner Sites.

Revoking Tracking Permission

You may revoke tracking permission at any time by navigating to your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > RecipeAI
  • Android: Settings > Privacy > Ads > Delete Advertising ID

Premium Subscribers

Premium subscribers are not shown advertisements. No advertising-related tracking occurs for premium subscribers, regardless of ATT permission status.
