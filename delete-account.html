We're sorry to see you go. If you've decided to delete your RecipeAI account, we want to make the process as simple and transparent as possible.

Account deletion is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, you will permanently lose access to:

  • All saved recipes (AI-generated and custom recipes)
  • Your favorite recipes collection
  • Recipe images and uploaded photos
  • Your personalization settings (dietary restrictions, allergies, cuisine preferences)
  • Recipe generation history
  • Your profile information and preferences

This data cannot be recovered after deletion.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

When you request account deletion, here's what will happen:

  • Immediate: Your account will be deactivated and you will no longer be able to log in
  • Within 30 days: Most of your personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems:
    • Profile information (name, email, avatar)
    • All saved recipes and images
    • User preferences and settings
    • Recipe generation history
  • Within 90 days: Backup copies will be permanently purged from our backup systems
  • What we may retain: For legal and compliance purposes, we may retain:
    • Transaction records (if you had a premium subscription) - up to 7 years as required by law
    • Anonymized, aggregated analytics data that cannot identify you
    • Records required for fraud prevention or legal compliance

Active Subscription?

If you have an active premium subscription:

  • Cancel your subscription first: We recommend canceling your subscription before deleting your account
  • Access the Stripe Customer Portal: You can manage your subscription through the in-app billing portal
  • No refunds: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription or provide a refund for unused time
  • We can help: If you have billing concerns, contact us at support@recipe-ai.io before deleting your account

‚úì Easiest Option: Delete In-App

You can delete your account directly from the RecipeAI mobile app:

  1. Open the RecipeAI app on your device
  2. Go to Settings or Profile
  3. Scroll down to find "Delete Account" or "Account Management"
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm deletion
  5. Your account will be immediately deactivated

This is the fastest way to delete your account.

Need Help Instead of Deletion?

Before you delete your account, consider if one of these options might help:

  • Having technical issues? Contact our support team at support@recipe-ai.io - we're here to help
  • Concerned about privacy? Review your Privacy Settings or adjust notification preferences
  • Need a break? You can simply log out and return anytime - your recipes will still be there
  • Subscription costs? Cancel your premium subscription and continue using the free tier

Your Privacy Rights

Account deletion is just one of your privacy rights. You also have the right to:

  • Access your data: Request a copy of all personal information we hold about you
  • Export your recipes: Download your recipes in a portable format before deletion
  • Correct your data: Update or fix any inaccurate information
  • Restrict processing: Limit how we use your data in certain circumstances

For more information about your privacy rights, see our Privacy Policy.
